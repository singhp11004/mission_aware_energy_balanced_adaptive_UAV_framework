Energy Consumption Rate by Mission Phase

This bar chart compares the average battery drain per round during different mission phases.

**Visualization Details**
- **X-Axis**: Mission Phases (PATROL, SURVEILLANCE, THREAT).
- **Y-Axis**: Average Battery Drain per Round (percentage points).
- **Colors**: Green (Patrol), Orange (Surveillance), Red (Threat).

**Interpretation**
- **PATROL**: Typically shows the lowest energy consumption as the swarm focuses on longevity and basic covering. Privacy measures are likely lighter.
- **SURVEILLANCE**: Moderate energy consumption. Standard sensing and operational traffic.
- **THREAT**: Should show the highest energy consumption due to aggressive privacy preservation techniques (high dummy traffic, complex routing) and frequent status updates.

**Correctness Check**
- The bars should logically follow the mission intensity: Patrol < Surveillance < Threat. If Threat phase has lower consumption than Patrol, verify if the dummy traffic or routing depth parameters are correctly configured for the Threat phase.
