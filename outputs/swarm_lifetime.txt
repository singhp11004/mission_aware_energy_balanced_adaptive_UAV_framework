Swarm Lifetime and Battery Status

This graph tracks the overall health of the swarm over the mission duration.

**Visualization Details**
- **Blue Line (Left Axis)**: Number of Active Drones.
- **Green Line (Right Axis)**: Mean Battery Level (%) of the swarm.
- **Red Dashed Line**: The 50% active threshold (critical failure point).

**Interpretation**
- **Active Drones**: Should remain constant at the total swarm size (e.g., 50) for most of the mission, then drop as batteries deplete.
- **Mean Battery**: Should decrease linearly or super-linearly over time.
- **Lifetime**: The point where the Blue line crosses a critical threshold (often defined as 50% or 30% surviving drones) marks the effective network lifetime.

**Correctness Check**
- If the Active Drones count drops immediately at the start, there is likely an initialization bug or extreme energy cost setting.
- If Mean Battery stays flat, the energy model is not decrementing correctly.
