Battery Distribution Analysis

This graph visualizes how energy is consumed across the UAV swarm during the mission.

**Left Panel: Battery Depletion Over Time (Sample Drones)**
- This line plot tracks the battery levels of specific individual drones (e.g., Drone 0, Drone 10, etc.) throughout the simulation rounds.
- **Interpretation**: steeper downward slopes indicate periods of high activity (sensing, relaying, or dummy traffic generation). Flatter lines indicate idle periods or low-energy modes.
- **Correctness Check**: Lines should monotonically decrease (or stay flat if not used). If any line goes up, that would indicate an error (unless recharging is implemented, which is not in this model).

**Right Panel: Battery Distribution at Different Points**
- These histograms show the spread of battery levels across the entire swarm at specific checkpoints (e.g., start, 25%, 50%, 75%, and end of simulation).
- **Interpretation**: A tight cluster implies balanced energy consumption (good load balancing). A wide spread indicates that some drones are being overworked while others are underutilized, potentially leading to premature network partition.
- **Correctness Check**: The distributions should shift left (lower battery) and potentially widen as the simulation progresses.
